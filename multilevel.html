Net Control Manager is a Create, Read, Update, Delete (CRUD) application used by Amateur Radio operators to document various net operations such as weather emergencies, club meetings, bike ride support and any other logging and/or reporting intensive communications support and management needs. A variety of reports can be created including mapping stations locations and other DHS/FEMA needs. Including the ICS-214 and ICS-309 reports and access to many others.
