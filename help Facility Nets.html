Facility Nets

Facility Nets are designed to support a series of predefined locations, such as hospitals, across your coverage area. Each facility is expected to have an appropriate number of volunteers assigned to it.

Setting Up a Facility Net

  1. Create a group profile at https://net-control.us/BuildNewGroup.php, ensuring to select 'FACILITY' as the group type.
  2. Prepare a comma-separated values (CSV) file containing the facilities and their respective locations (latitude and longitude).
  3. Upload the CSV file to the NCM application.
  4. Configure the Facility Net settings, specifying the number of volunteers needed for each facility.

Managing Facility Nets

During a Facility Net, the Net Control Station (NCS) calls the net by facility name. Each station logs in, and their check-in is automatically associated with the corresponding facility. The logger can easily indicate if a station is physically present at the facility or reporting from another location.

The Facility Net view provides a sorted list of facilities and their assigned volunteers, making it easier to track the status and location of each station.

For more information or assistance with setting up a Facility Net, please contact the NCM support team.
